Danish Business Management Platform

The Danish Business Management Platform is a SaaS solution built to automate and centralize essential business operations. It enhances operational efficiency, from sales processes to customer analytics, empowering businesses to make informed, data-driven decisions.

🔧 Technology Stack

  • Backend: NestJS · TypeScript · TypeORM
  • Frontend: React · TypeScript · Chakra UI
  • Database: MySQL
  • DevOps & Security: Docker · mTLS (Ubuntu)
  • Advanced Analytics: Machine Learning for predictive analytics

Core Platform Features

  • Secure Access Control: Role-based authentication with tiered permissions.
  • Customer & Product Management: Efficiently manage customer information, transaction history, and product catalogs with pricing tiers.
  • Advanced Invoicing: Create, track, and export invoices with customizable reporting formats (Excel/PDF).
  • Integrated Payments: Record and track various payment methods (e.g., cash, cheque, bank transfers) alongside invoice reconciliation.
  • Dynamic Reporting & Analytics: Comprehensive and exportable analytics reports, visual dashboards, and data-driven insights to monitor business performance.
  • Predictive Insights: Utilize machine learning to forecast sales trends, analyze customer behavior, and detect anomalies.
  • Enhanced Security: mTLS-secured platform with restricted desktop client access to ensure maximum data protection.
